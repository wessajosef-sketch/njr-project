About Not Just Recycling

We're dedicated to making the world a cleaner, greener place through innovative recycling solutions

Our Mission

At Not Just Recycling, our mission is to transform the way people think about waste. We believe that every piece of recyclable material has value and potential for a new life.

  • Encourage individuals and businesses to actively participate in recycling programs

  • Provide convenient collection services for recyclable materials

  • Reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills

  • Create awareness about the importance of recycling and environmental conservation

  • Reward and incentivize sustainable behavior through our program

Plastic
